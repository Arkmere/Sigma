# Monetisation

Ticket 1 implements no payments or payment SDKs.

## Direction
- Free personal use should remain genuinely useful indefinitely: one personal profile, core measurements, common clothing/footwear sizes and local-first storage.
- Preferred full unlock is a fair one-time purchase covering Family creation, managed profiles, adult connections, sharing, broader catalogues, conversions, brand fit, history and privacy controls.
- A possible separate one-time Extended Family upgrade may support unusually large groups.

Approximate future ranges such as 6 standard profiles and 20 extended profiles are illustrative, not final caps.

Avoid subscriptions unless recurring costs justify them, arbitrary microtransactions, deliberately crippled free tiers and nickel-and-diming.
