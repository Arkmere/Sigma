# Sharing and Consent

Future adult flow: Person A initiates connection; Person B explicitly accepts; connection exists; no data is visible by default; each owner grants specific access.

Sharing may be granted for an entire profile, category, specific measurement, size records without raw measurements, or specific brand/product fit records. The UI must always answer who can see this, what can they see, who granted access, and can it be revoked.

Revocation must be explicit, reliable and auditable. Ownership remains with the profile/data owner.

Managed child profiles are more restrictive: maintained under a parent/guardian, initially shared only inside Family structures, no arbitrary public sharing or casual public links. The age transition policy is unresolved and must not be hard-coded.
